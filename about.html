About Us

Mea Culpa is an intermediary platform between seller and buyers. We emphasise on competition and believe in free market concept. Based on price concluded after the auction, we help to fetch digital agreement between parties involved ie; buyer and seller. So that the buyers can purchase the product from the seller or the registered shop or dealer directly at the price decided from the auction conducted through Mea Culpa platform.
